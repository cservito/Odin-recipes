Vanilla Frozen Yogurt

Description:

This is an inredibly simple and healthy desert option for a nice, relaxing evening at home!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of non-fat Greek yogurt
  • 2/3 cups of white sugar
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Stir together the yogurt, sugar, and vanilla in a bowl until the sugar has been dissolved.
  2. Cover and refrigerate for an hour.
  3. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and freeze accordingly until it has a "soft-serve" viscosity.
  4. Transfer the mixture to a plastic container and seal with plastic wrap.
  5. Allow the frozen yogurt to set in the freezer for at least 2 hours or overnight.
