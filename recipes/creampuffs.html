Cream Puffs

Description:

Enjoy these classic French deserts, a choux pastry ball filled with cream, in a very simple recipe you can do at home!

Ingredients

  • 3.5 ounces of instant vanilla pudding
  • 2 cups of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 4 large eggs

Steps

  1. Mix the instant vanilla pudding, cream, and milk, and cover and refrigerate.
  2. Preheat the oven to 425 F.
  3. Bring the water and butter to a boil in a large pot.
  4. Stir in the flour and salt until the mixture becomes dough.
  5. Take the dough out and using a wooden spoon or standing mixer, beat in the eggs one at a time.
  6. Use a tablespoon to drop a ball of the dough onto an ungreased baking sheet, spaced about a couple of inches apart.
  7. Bake in the oven for 20 to 25 minutes, or until the center is dry.
  8. When the pastries are cooled, split and fill them with the cream using a pastry bag and serve.
