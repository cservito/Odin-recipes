Pumpkin Pie with Vanilla Meringue

Description:

This recipe adds something deliciously simple to a classic desert!

Ingredients

Pumpkin Pie

  • A 9-inch unbaked, pie crust
  • A 15-oz can of pumpkin puree
  • A 14-oz can of sweetened, condensed milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp of pumpkin pie spice

Vanilla Meringue

  • 1 cup of granulated sugar
  • 5 large egg whites
  • 1/4 tsp crean of tartar
  • 1 pinch of Kosher salt
  • 2 tsp of pure vanilla extract

Steps

Pumpkin Pie

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F.
  2. Mix together the pumpking puree, condensed milk, eggs, and pumpkin pie spice in a large bowl.
  3. Mix the batter until it's smooth, then pour into the unbacked pie crust.
  4. Place into oven for 15 mintues, then reduce heat to 350 degrees F and let pie bake until the filling is set.

Vanilla Meringue

  1. Mix together the sugar, egg whites, cream of tartar, and salt in the top pan of a double boiler or in a heatproof bowl set over a medium, heavy-bottomed saucepan filled with a few inches of simmer water, but be sure the bottom of the bowl does not touch the water.
  2. Stir the mixture using a rubber spatula over medium heat, scarping the edges of the bowl until the sugar is totally melted and a candy thermomete reads 160 degrees F once placed in the center of the mixture.
  3. Pour the mixture into the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with a whisk attachment and whisk on low for 1 mintue, then increase the spead to medium-high and whisk for 10 minutes, and then add the vanilla extract and continue to whisk until combined.
  4. Scoop the meringue immediately onto the pumpkin pie and serve.
